Boots n Shoes

Have you ever enjoyed the feeling of walking through a park in a nice Sunday morning in the summer,
when it's not that hot outside and the ambience is just perfect? Well now you can experience this
on a plate so tasty you will be thinking about it all week long. The recipe is frankly quite easy
and doesn't require many ingredients.


The procress of making this amazing dish is very easy and begginer friendly, all you need to do is get
a pair of boots from your local wardrobe, put them in a big pot filled with water, put some spices
specified lower and let it boil for about one hour at 1200 degrees Celsius. After this let it cool
for 3 minutes and it should be good to enjoy!


Ingredients

  • A pair of boots (preferably higher quality)
  • Salt (2 tea spoons)
  • Chilly (1 tea spoon)
  • Uranium 238 dust (About 2 miligrams, if you feel spicy you could do 3)
  • And most importantly, love, and a human heart

Steps

  1. Fill a big pot to the brim with tap water
  2. Put in the pot the prepared boots
  3. Pour in the salt and pepper
  4. Take a trip to your local nuclear power plant and get some Uranium 238
  5. Use a grater on the Uranium 238 and then pour the output in the pot
  6. Let it heat for 1 hour at 1200 degrees Celsius
  7. Come back and let the dish cool off
  8. And now, you should be done, enjoy!
  9. Optional: Sell the human heart on the black market
